Welcome

Hey there! Thanks for checking out our first issue of room36. We are a collective of artists from different backgrounds that found each other during our time in university. We all met in room 36 of the communications building. Since then, we've become deeply interested in one another's work. We wanted a way to stay together after university and share our creative energies jointly. The result is what you see here. Take a look!
